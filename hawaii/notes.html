Activities

  • Charlie Young's beach (restrooms, lifeguard), in Kihei (Key-Hey!!)
  • Learn to surf, The Cove in Kihei is a good place to learn, also has surf rentals
  • Black Rock Beach (in Ka'anapali, Hawaii): Sand castles + snorkeling
  • Possible alternative to island hopping (avoiding hotel check-out, rental car, etc..) Mokulele Air Tours, $500/person, Leave from Kapalua Airport (near Ka'anapali)
  • Hula Grill in Ka'anapali
  • Twin Falls Maui Waterfall, hint: there are several waterfalls (but most people only know about the first one on the trail more), leave a nice donation, 1 hour drive from Lahaina (via Road to Hana), hiking trail has port-a-potties, bring the right kind of shoes for hiking (1.3 mile trail) and wading/swimming, bring bug repellent, stop at other sites along the way (Baldwin Beach Park, etc.)
  • Lahaina Stables???
  • Day trip to the Big Island
  • Kahekili Beach (Airport beach): north of Black Rock Beach, less crowded, good snorkeling, Caution: May be polluted!!
  • Kapalua Bay: "Shallow, diverse reef is perfect for beginners" top 10 spots
  • Napili Bay: Good for beginners and advanced
  • Atlantis submarine ride
  • Honolua Bay Marine Life Conservation District: Spot for experienced surfers, 20 minute drive north of Lahaina, free parking lot (21.011896,-156.636854), snorkel sail (catamaran from from Lahaina Harbor)
  • Road to Hana: Drive part of the way to Ke'anae Peninsula and then return. (Driving the whole way is probably too long with kids...)
  • Baby Beach: Good for kids, Lagoon
